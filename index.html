Duck Facts!!!

Ducks are aquatic birds known for their broad, flat bills and webbed feet, which help them swim. They are found in both freshwater and saltwater habitats and are known for their distinctive quacking sounds. Ducks are omnivores, feeding on plants, insects, and small fish. They are social creatures that often form groups, known as flocks, and are known for their migratory behavior, traveling long distances between breeding and wintering grounds.



Things Ducks Love:

  • Company (duck friends!)
  • A huge pond
  • Lots of water
  • Grass and fish (and greens)

Ducks love swimming in ponds.

Things Ducks HATE:

  • The ocean
  • Bread
  • Strong scents
  • Loneliness

Bread is not good for ducks.
